Best Brownies Ever

By Jorge Zavala

This is a simple recipe for the best brownies you will ever eat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • dry ingredients:
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup flour

Directions

  1. Eat your vegetables first.
  2. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  3. Mix oil and sugar until well blended.
    1. Add eggs and vanilla
    2. stir until blended.
  4. Mix dry ingredients in seperate bowl.
  5. Stir dry ingredients into the oil/sugar bowl.
  6. Pour into greased 9x9 square pan.
  7. Bake for 30 20 minutes.
  8. Cool completely before cutting.

As my father once said, These are good brownies.
