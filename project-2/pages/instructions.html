Brownies

Ingredients Instructions
Step 1 Preheat oven to 350oF.
Step 2
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
 Mix oil and sugar until well blended.
Step 3
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
 Add eggs and vanilla. Stir until well blended.
Step 4
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup flour
 Mix dry ingredients in seperate bowl.
Step 5 Stir dry ingredients into the oil/sugar bowl.
Step 6 Pour into greased 9x9 square pan.
Step 7 Bake for 30 20 minutes.
Step 8 Cool completely before cutting.
