About The Recipe

Brownies have a lot of history behind them and have been improved to perfection. This delicious, baked, chocolate dessert is known among almost all Americans as a top sweet treat ever created.

The reason I chose to create this brownie instructable was because I couldn't remember the last time I ever made brownies from scratch. As one of America's top desserts, I understood that when I looked up recipes, there would be tons of different variations. I decided to go for a very simple, yet authentic, approach to the recipe.

I hope this recipe brings many smiles to people's faces.
